❓ What are medium identity threats?
⚠️ Fake job offers, fake scholarships, and unsafe websites.
❓ Scholarship scams ask for?
💰 Money or fees.
❓ What to do with a strange email?
📩 Show it to parents/teachers, don’t click links.
❓ How do scams trick kids?
⚠️ They use urgent messages and fake logos.
❓ Golden rule online?
🛑 Stop – Think – Check.
❓ Repeated offers mean?
⚠️ They are scams.
❓ Are all websites safe?
🚫 No, unsafe sites can steal info.
❓ If unsure about a message?
🔍 Ask a trusted adult.
❓ What can scams cause?
⚠️ Loss of money or accounts.
❓ Best defense?
🛡 Check with parents/teachers before clicking.
