⚠️ Phishing Awareness – Medium Level

Can you identify online scams? 🔍

Cards explored: 0 / 8 ⭐

📧 Your school email says your account is locked. What should you do?
⚠️ Report it to your teacher. Do not click the link in the email.
🌐 A website has many spelling mistakes. Is it safe?
⚠️ No. Fake websites often have spelling errors. Close it and tell an adult.
📥 Someone online sends you a file to download. Should you open it?
🚫 No. Files from strangers may contain viruses or scams.
🔗 The link says "bank.com" but opens another website. What should you do?
⚠️ Do not click it. The link may lead to a fake website.
👨‍🏫 Who should you report suspicious emails to?
🙂 Tell a trusted adult, parent, or teacher.
⚠️ What are common signs of phishing?
Urgent messages, spelling mistakes, and strange links.
🔒 Why should you avoid clicking unknown links?
They may steal your personal information or passwords.
📧 Where can phishing happen online?
Phishing can happen through emails, fake websites, and messages.
