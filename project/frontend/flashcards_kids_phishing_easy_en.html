❓ What is phishing?
⚠️ Phishing is an online trick used to steal secrets like passwords.
❓ What rule should you follow before clicking?
🛑 Stop – Think – Ask a trusted adult.
❓ Who should you tell if something feels strange online?
🙂 Tell a trusted adult like your parents or teacher.
❓ Is it safe to share passwords?
🔒 No. Passwords must always stay secret.
❓ What are signs of phishing?
⚠️ Spelling mistakes, strange links, urgent messages.
❓ Example of phishing in games?
🎮 Fake offers like “Get 1000 free coins”.
❓ Where can phishing happen?
📱 Emails, games, and social media.
❓ What should you do if a site looks odd?
⚠️ Close it and tell a trusted adult.
