❓ What are medium-level scams?
⚠️ Fake job offers, loan scams, phishing emails pretending to be banks.
❓ What do loan scams usually ask for?
💰 Fees or advance payments.
❓ What should you do with phishing emails?
📩 Verify with your bank, don’t click links.
❓ How do scams trick people?
⚠️ They use urgent messages and official-looking logos.
❓ What is the golden rule online?
🛑 Stop – Think – Check.
❓ Repeated job offers online mean?
⚠️ They are scams.
❓ Are all bank emails safe?
🚫 No, some may be scams.
❓ What to do if unsure about a loan offer?
🔍 Ask your bank, don’t pay fees.
❓ What do scams cause?
⚠️ Financial loss and stress.
❓ Best defense against scams?
🛡 Check official sites and stay cautious.
