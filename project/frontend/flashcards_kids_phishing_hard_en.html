⚠️ Online Scams Awareness – Hard Level (Adults)

Stay alert! Can you spot the scams? 🔍

Cards explored: 0 / 10 ⭐

💰 An investment site promises huge returns quickly. What’s happening?
⚠️ It may be an investment fraud. High returns with no risk are a scam sign.
📞 A caller says they are tech support and need remote access. Safe or scam?
🚫 Scam. Real companies don’t ask for remote access unexpectedly.
🆔 What does identity theft mean?
⚠️ Stealing personal details like bank info, ID numbers, or passwords.
📩 Best action if you suspect fraud?
✅ Report to authorities immediately, don’t send money or details.
💸 Investment scams usually promise?
⚠️ High returns with little or no risk.
📞 True or False: Unknown callers can be trusted.
❌ False. Never trust unknown callers asking for details.
🛡 Best defense against scams?
✅ Verify with official sources before acting.
💻 Tech support scams often ask for?
⚠️ Remote access to your computer or device.
⚠️ What can scams cause?
💔 Financial loss and emotional stress.
🛑 Golden rule online?
Stop – Think – Check before clicking or sharing info.
