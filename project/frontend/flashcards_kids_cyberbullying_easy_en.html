❓ What is cyberbullying?
⚠️ Cyberbullying is when someone hurts others online with mean words or actions.
❓ Where can cyberbullying happen?
📱 On social media, games, or class group chats.
❓ What are signs of cyberbullying?
⚠️ Mean comments, teasing, exclusion, or fake posts.
❓ Is it safe to reply to bullies?
🚫 No. Don’t reply. Block or report instead.
❓ Who should you tell if bullied online?
🙂 Tell a trusted adult like parents or teachers.
❓ Example of cyberbullying in games?
🎮 Players sending mean messages or excluding you from play.
❓ What rule should you follow online?
🛑 Stop – Think – Ask a trusted adult.
❓ What should you do if a post feels hurtful?
⚠️ Report it, block the bully, and talk to an adult.
