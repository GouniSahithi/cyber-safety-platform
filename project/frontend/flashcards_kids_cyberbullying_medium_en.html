⚠️ Cyberbullying Awareness – Medium Level

Can you spot online bullying? 🔍

Cards explored: 0 / 8 ⭐

👤 A fake account is made in your name. What should you do?
⚠️ Take screenshots and report it to parents, teachers, or the platform.
📱 Friends share mean posts about you in a group. How should you react?
🚫 Do not fight back. Save evidence and tell a trusted adult.
🌐 Someone spreads false rumors online. Is it safe to ignore?
⚠️ No. Rumors can spread fast. Report and block the bully.
👨‍🏫 Who should you report cyberbullying to?
🙂 Parents, teachers, or school counselors.
⚠️ What are common signs of cyberbullying?
Mean comments, fake accounts, exclusion, and group bullying.
🤝 Peer pressure makes you share personal photos. Is this safe?
🚫 No. Never share private photos under pressure.
🔒 Why should you avoid replying to bullies?
It can make things worse. Block and report instead.
📧 Where can cyberbullying happen online?
On social media, gaming chats, and school groups.
